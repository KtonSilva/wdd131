About Me

My name is Kleyton L Silva. I was born in Brazil and live with my family in Recife-PE

I have a degree in Graphic Design from Faculdade Guararapes and a postgraduate degree in Digital Design from Estácio. I am currently in the third semester of an online course in English, certified in "Web & Computer Programming," at Brigham Young University.

I have experience in creating advertising materials, graphic prints, digital art, and visual identity.

I particularly enjoy working with Web Programming, Layout Design, Video Editing, and Image Editing. My main areas of interest are: Games, Hardware, Technology, and Pop Culture.
